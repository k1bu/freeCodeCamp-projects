Claude Shannon

Architect of the Information Age

Harbinger of the silicon world
  • Born on April 30th, 1916 in Petoskey, Michigan
  • Childhood hero: Thomas Edison
  • Worked as a messenger for Western Union in high school
  • Apolitical and atheist
  • Alma mater: University of Michigan
  • Bachelor degrees in electrical engineering and mathematics
  • His master's thesis forms basis for all electronic digital computing
  • Receives Ph.D from MIT in 1940
  • Joins Bell Labs during WW2
  • Creates robotic mouse 'Theseus' which could teach itself to navigate a maze
  • Estimates the game-tree complexity of chess at 10120
  • Shannon's maxim: The enemy knows the system
